About the Artist

My name is Sydney Cornelius, or Riven and Wormz online. I am a college student and I do art as a hobby. I'm delighted that you're interested in viewing my commissions!

I am a smaller artist, so I apologize for the smaller portfolio, but I'd be delighted if you stuck around, commissioned me, or even just sent a message to learn more about me and my work.

Interests and Specialties

My art is primarily humans and humanoids, though I have been learning to draw ponies like in My Little Pony and cats.

Experience

As I have previously mentioned, I am a hobbyist, so I am not an art student. I have been drawing since middle school and have a pretty decent amount of experience.

Contact

If you'd like to get ahold of me, I'd prefer to be reached through my email: W0rmzfordinn3r@gmail.com.

Commission Guidelines

What I will do

  • Ponies

  • Groups

What I won't do

  • NSFW
  • Mechs

  • Scenes or landscapes

I will not be accepting works of AI for references when it comes to commissions. I do not support AI art and request that you respect this.

I reserve the right to refuse service if I deem treatment unkind or the work environment too hostile for me to safely work.
